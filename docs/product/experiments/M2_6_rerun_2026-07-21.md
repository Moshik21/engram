### NEW finding — flag-ON latency cost (flip blocker)

| arm | flag | median recall |
|---|---|---|
| A / A_r / A2 | OFF | **0.36 / 0.38 / 0.43s** |
| E / B1 / B2 | ON | **3.02 / 3.14 / 2.89s** |

The flag itself costs **+2.6s median per recall (~8x)** on this brain — paid
even with a completely EMPTY usage store (arm E). Cause:
`_apply_episode_usage_tiebreaker` (M5.1, `retrieval/pipeline.py`) awaits
`graph_store.get_episode_cue()` **sequentially per episode/cue candidate**
(~40 native reads x ~65ms). Flipping today would take the live shell's
just-restored 438ms recall to ~3s and burn most of the continuity gate's
margin. Prerequisite fix: batch the cue reads (one `find_cues_by_episode_ids`
call) or skip composition when the cue substrate has zero used-tier events.
